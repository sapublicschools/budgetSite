Budget Portal

2017-2018
Welcome! The purpose of this website is to provide an approachable and thorough introduction to the budget of South Amboy School District, a K-12 public school system that serves approximately 1200 students in pre-kindergarten through 12th grade. Official information can be found here, but this website serves as an introduction for those who are not familiar with budgets in general.

2017-2018 Revenues Chart

The vast majority of our funding comes from two sources: State Aid and Local Tax Levy.
